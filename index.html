Frequently Asked Questions

Event design and event planning work hand in hand to deliver a successful event.Events by Knight offer tailor made events. This means we incorporate both event design and event planning to create that perfect event.

The MJCC does not permit outside food and beverage. While an individual item i.e. bottle of water is ok, Items in bulk such as a case of water or a cooler with food and snacks is not allowed on property and will have to be consumed outside of the venue. Please contact the event organizer if you have further questions.

Daily parking rate ranges $15-30; due to demand and location, the fees vary between parking garages and events. Feel free to inquire about a specific event's pricing. NOTE: Accessible parking and motorcycle parking are also subject to the same fees.
